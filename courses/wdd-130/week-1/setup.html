Accounts and Software Setup

For this class you will need to signup for several free online accounts and install the required code editor. Do this as soon as possible. Please note that a Chromebook is not acceptable for this class.

Assignment

  1. Signup for a free GitHub account (if you do not have one already) with your student email. As a student you will be given free access to a Pro Account. Once your account has been created visit GitHub Education and click on the Get Benifits button to activate your pro account.

  2. Signup for a free Glitch account (if you do not have one already) with your student email OR by linking to your GitHub account. If you create a new account with your email you will have to verify your account by clicking on a link sent to this email. sometimes the BYUI spam filters reject emails from Glitch, if your unable to complete the Glitch verification because of this you may create an account with your personal email; it is recommenced to link Glitch with your GitHub account to save your from all this hassle.

  3. Install VS Code on your machine; your instructor will demo in class how to install any extensions you need for this class.

    • For the privacy conscious you can install and use VS Codium instead; you will need to figure out the correct install for your machine on your own.

  4. Copy and complete the following template by replacing the text within the brackets with the requested information; please remove the brackets in your submission. You may submit this as a Text Entry or Upload to this assignment in Canvas to prove you have completed these steps.

    Grading:

    Submit the following template with the correct changes as a Text Entry or Upload (create a word document) to this assignment in Canvas to prove you have completed these steps. You will need to use a screen capture tool to take a picture of your screen and replace the placeholder image in your submission by copy-pasting your new image into the submission. Please do not submit a URL to your screenshot.

Template

My GitHub profile URL is: [URL to your GitHub profile. Example: https://github.com/blizzardengle]
