Graphic Design

Graphic Design is a broad disciple with many job roles and skills. For this course we focus on basic graphic design principles that are useful for developers. In this assignment you will learn how color, layout, images, composition, typography, branding, and more relate to graphic design. If you are interested in perusing a Graphic Design career the skills will we cover in this assignment are the foundations for a good web designer. Some of this skills are also useful for digital and print designers.
