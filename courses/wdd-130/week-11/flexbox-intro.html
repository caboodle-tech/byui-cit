Introduction to CSS Flexbox

The final project template that you have been provided for this course uses a mix of CSS grid — which you have already learned — and CSS flexbox. This week your instructor will demonstrate the basics of CSS flexbox in class. This assignment will help you learn flexbox in greater depth. As you study the resources below and complete the required assignment make sure to note any questions you have. Coming to class with questions will allow your instructor to demonstrate the answers live in class.

Assignment

  1. Study

    Basic Concepts of Flexbox is an article written for MDN Web Doc that will teach you the fundamentals of flexbox. Please read this article carefully, you will be quizzed on it later in the week.

    Another great resource you should review is A Complete Guide to Flexbox by CSS-Tricks. You will not be quizzed on this article but you may find it helpful for your final project site and/or future classes.

  2. Watch

  3. Practice

    To practice flexbox you will need to complete all 24 levels of Flexbox Froggy. Remember to do all your work on the same computer, otherwise your progress will not be saved. Do not click the next button on level 24! There is a bug that will wipe your progress. Make sure to take your screenshot before completing level 24.

    Another great resource you should review (that is optional) is Flexbox Defense. Using your flexbox skills you will place defensive towers to defend yourself from invading enemies.
